Salt Lake City Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

Salt Lake City Chamber of Commerce

The name represents the city in which the Chamber of Commerce operates.

Site Purpose

The Salt Lake City Chamber of Commerce is a coalition which supports the local community via encouraging and aiding local businesses, creating new networking oppertunities, and keeping our community's prosperity at the forefront of policy decisions.

Scenario 1:

Events this month

What events are being hosted this month, and when and where are they?

Scenario 2:

How to commend a local business.

How can an individual give some form of commendation or recognition to a local business that's gone above and beyond for them?

Scenario 3:

How do I find information on past accomplishments of the Salt Lake City Chamber of Commerce?

How can a curious community-member find more information on what the Salt Lake City Chamber of Commerce has done for the community and businesses?

Color Schema: Primary

Primary Page Color

The purple chosen feels like it represents the feel of the chamber as a trustworthy and upstanding part of the society. This scheme used on the site plan and will be the primary color for the site.

Color Schema: Accent

Accent Color.

The accent color will be used for places like the navigation bar and additional information as a way of bringing out contrast with the site theme, as detailed in the color guide provided.

Typography Examples

Font 1: Lato

To be used primarily for the bulk of the site as an easily-readible option.

Font 2: Kalam

To be used as a stylistic accent for the navigation bar.
